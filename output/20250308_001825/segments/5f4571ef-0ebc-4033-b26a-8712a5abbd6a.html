Mystery in Glenmoor

In the sprawling coastal town of Glenmoor, beneath foggy skies and amidst cobblestone streets winding like veins, a shadowy mystery unraveled, quietly ensnaring every resident in its intricate web. At its heart lay the enigmatic disappearance of Eleanor Blythe, the cherished heiress of the grand and ancient Blythe family estate.
