Madame Agnes' Tea Salon

Every Sunday afternoon, Detective Croft meticulously watched interactions unfold at Madame Agnes’ elegant tea salon, a neutral ground where Glenmoor's socialites and gentry discreetly mingled. Madame Agnes, once a celebrated Parisian opera singer known throughout Europe and Eleanor's close confidante, possessed an enviable ability to keep secrets—but even her practiced composure faltered, barely masking a deep distress since Eleanor's disappearance.
