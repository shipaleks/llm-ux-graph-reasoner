The Clockmaker's Tale

Inspector Helena Cross, an insightful investigator burdened by insomnia and a fascination with riddles, arrived to unravel the convoluted case. She first learned of the compass from Ezra Hollow, the village clockmaker, whose shop housed innumerable ticking clocks, each showing a different, inexplicable time. Ezra claimed the compass vanished during a midnight blizzard, exactly as his clocks simultaneously halted.
