Blacksmith's Secret Commission

Meanwhile, Alder Shaw, the village blacksmith known for forging peculiar objects upon request, had been commissioned by an anonymous client to create an intricate replica of the compass. Alder, tight-lipped and wary, confided only in Maeve Thorn, a reclusive widow who tended the local library stocked with obscure manuscripts filled with riddles and paradoxes.
