The Inn and Guests

Ezra’s sister, Vera Hollow, maintained the village inn, often hosting guests with enigmatic purposes. Among them was Lucian Greer, a wandering herbalist obsessed with exotic plants said to bloom only under moonlight. Lucian's journals, filled with cryptic botanical sketches intertwined with celestial charts, hinted at hidden meanings and were coveted by Bridget Locke, the local historian whose fascination with ancient legends bordered on obsession.
