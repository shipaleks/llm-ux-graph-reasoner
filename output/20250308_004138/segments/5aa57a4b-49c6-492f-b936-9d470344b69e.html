Mystery in Eldermoor

In the isolated mountain village of Eldermoor, where snow perpetually blanketed steep rooftops and ancient pines whispered endlessly, a peculiar mystery befell the quiet inhabitants. At the center was the disappearance of a mysterious artifact known only as the Veiled Compass, a relic rumored to guide its holder through impossible paths.
