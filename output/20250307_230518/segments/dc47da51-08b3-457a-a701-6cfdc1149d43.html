Croft's Investigation Begins

Detective Oliver Croft, a reserved and contemplative man with perpetually tired eyes and a worn leather notebook always tucked into his coat, was summoned to investigate. His first clue emerged in the form of an antique locket found by a street musician named Simon Bellows. Simon, once a famed concert pianist whose melodies enchanted Europe’s elite, had been disgraced after a scandal involving Celia Rhodes, Eleanor's former governess. Celia herself vanished years prior under a cloud of rumors surrounding a supposed affair with Eleanor’s domineering father, Gregory Blythe.
