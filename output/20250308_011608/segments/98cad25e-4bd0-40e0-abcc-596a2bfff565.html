Masquerade Ball at Ravenscroft

On a stormy autumn evening, Lord Alistair Ravenscroft hosted an extravagant masquerade ball at the foreboding Ravenscroft Manor, a gothic estate perched atop a rugged cliff overlooking the sea. Known for its winding corridors, secret chambers, and whispered tales of ghostly apparitions, the manor was filled with hidden mysteries, ancient relics, and layers of intrigue.
