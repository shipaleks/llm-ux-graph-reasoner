Discovery in the Attic

The final, revelatory unraveling occurred in the dusty attic above Harriet’s bookstore, hidden behind towering stacks of forgotten, unsold books. There, Detective Croft found Celia Rhodes, alive and weathered by guilt, silence, and years spent hiding in shadows. Celia, tormented by memories she refused to fully disclose, remained cryptic and sorrowful, leaving Croft with more questions than answers.
