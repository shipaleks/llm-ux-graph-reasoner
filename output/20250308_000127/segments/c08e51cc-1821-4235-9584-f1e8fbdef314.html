Intrigue and Relationships

Gregory, stern and elusive, ran a vast shipping empire that spanned continents, closely assisted by his trusted but morally ambiguous accountant, Nathaniel Price. Nathaniel, known for his precise calculations and emotionless demeanor, carried secrets heavy enough to bend even his rigid frame. Nathaniel’s nephew, Jacob Price, passionate and impetuous, secretly courted Eleanor despite Gregory’s fierce disapproval. Jacob’s letters, filled with poetic declarations yet tinged with desperation, were frequently intercepted by Prudence Hartwell, the Blythe family's meticulous and judgmental housekeeper, who secretly harbored a profound and unrequited affection for Gregory.
