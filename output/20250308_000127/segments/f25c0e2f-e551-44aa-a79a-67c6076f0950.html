Investigating Blythe Family Secrets

Amelia Cole, an ambitious young journalist with an unyielding thirst for truth, tirelessly chased leads regarding the myriad secrets and scandals enveloping the Blythe family. Amelia was discreetly aided by her reluctant informant, Dr. Tobias Sinclair, the family physician burdened by a dark knowledge acquired decades earlier when treating Gregory’s late wife, Margaret. Margaret’s death, originally declared natural, haunted Tobias deeply, his conscience compelling him towards Amelia despite the risks.
